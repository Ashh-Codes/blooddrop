We, the youth, can make India Blood Sufficient

Drops is India’s largest youth-led organization that works with colleges, corporates, and RWAs to help the blood banks be blood sufficient.
We provide a 360° solution to the problem: from motivating people to donate blood,
to helping them donate via camps and finally helping anyone in dire need via a helpline.
